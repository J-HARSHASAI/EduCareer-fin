MTech in VLSI & Embedded Systems

Master the design of integrated circuits and embedded systems

Course Description

The MTech in VLSI & Embedded Systems focuses on designing integrated circuits and embedded systems for applications in IoT, automotive, and consumer electronics. This program provides in-depth knowledge of VLSI design, embedded system architecture, and hardware-software integration, preparing graduates for specialized roles in the semiconductor and electronics industry.

Relevant Subjects

  • VLSI Design
  • Embedded System Design
  • Digital System Design
  • Microprocessors
  • System-on-Chip (SoC) Design
  • Analog and Mixed-Signal Design
  • Hardware Description Languages (HDL)

Required Skills

  • Proficiency in hardware description languages (VHDL/Verilog)
  • Circuit design and simulation
  • Embedded programming (C/C++)
  • Knowledge of microcontrollers and microprocessors
  • Debugging and testing of hardware systems
  • Familiarity with EDA (Electronic Design Automation) tools
  • Problem-solving and analytical skills
