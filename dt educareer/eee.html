BTech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Power the future with cutting-edge electrical systems and innovative electronics

Course Description

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) focuses on the study, design, and application of electrical systems, power generation, and electronic devices. This field equips students with the skills to innovate in areas such as power systems, control systems, telecommunications, and embedded systems, driving advancements in technology and energy solutions.

Relevant Subjects

  • Power Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Digital Electronics
  • Analog Electronics
  • Signals and Systems
  • Electrical Machines

Required Skills

  • Circuit Design (MATLAB, PSpice)
  • Power System Analysis
  • Microcontroller Programming
  • PLC Programming
  • Signal Processing
  • Problem Solving
  • Team Collaboration

Job Roles After Completion

Power Systems Engineer

Job Description

Power Systems Engineers design, maintain, and optimize electrical power generation, transmission, and distribution systems. They use tools like MATLAB and ETAP to analyze power flow, ensure grid stability, and integrate renewable energy sources. They also ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards while collaborating with utility companies and project teams.

Relevant Subjects

Power Systems Electrical Machines

Required Skills

Power System Analysis MATLAB ETAP Grid Management

Career Path

Junior Power Systems Engineer Power Systems Engineer Senior Power Systems Engineer Power Systems Consultant

Salary Range

₹6-22 LPA

Industry Demand

High demand in energy, utilities, and renewable energy sectors due to increasing focus on sustainable power solutions.

Companies Providing These Jobs

NTPC Tata Power Adani Power Siemens Schneider Electric

Work Environment

Work in offices for system design and analysis, with site visits to power plants or substations for inspections and implementation.
Control Systems Engineer

Job Description

Control Systems Engineers design and implement systems to control machinery and processes in industries like manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive. They develop control algorithms, program PLCs, and use tools like MATLAB and Simulink to optimize system performance. They also troubleshoot and maintain control systems to ensure reliability.

Relevant Subjects

Control Systems Signals and Systems

Required Skills

PLC Programming MATLAB/Simulink Control Algorithms System Integration

Career Path

Junior Control Systems Engineer Control Systems Engineer Senior Control Systems Engineer Automation Consultant

Salary Range

₹6-20 LPA

Industry Demand

Strong demand in automation, manufacturing, and robotics due to the need for precise control systems in modern industries.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Siemens Rockwell Automation Honeywell ABB L&T Technology Services

Work Environment

Mix of office-based design and programming with on-site work to install and test control systems in industrial settings.
Electronics Design Engineer

Job Description

Electronics Design Engineers develop and test electronic circuits and systems for devices like consumer electronics, medical equipment, and telecommunications systems. They use tools like PSpice and Altium Designer to design circuit boards, perform simulations, and ensure compliance with industry standards. They also collaborate with product development teams to integrate electronics into final products.

Relevant Subjects

Digital Electronics Analog Electronics

Required Skills

Circuit Design PCB Design PSpice/Altium Testing and Validation

Career Path

Junior Electronics Engineer Electronics Design Engineer Senior Electronics Engineer Electronics Design Lead

Salary Range

₹5-20 LPA

Industry Demand

High demand in consumer electronics, telecommunications, and IoT industries due to rapid advancements in electronic devices.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Samsung Texas Instruments Qualcomm Bosch Philips

Work Environment

Primarily lab-based for circuit design and testing, with office work for simulations and collaboration with product teams.
Embedded Systems Engineer

Job Description

Embedded Systems Engineers design and program embedded systems for applications like IoT devices, automotive electronics, and medical equipment. They write firmware, program microcontrollers, and integrate hardware and software components to ensure system functionality. They also perform debugging and testing to meet performance requirements.

Relevant Subjects

Digital Electronics Signals and Systems

Required Skills

Microcontroller Programming Firmware Development RTOS Debugging

Career Path

Junior Embedded Engineer Embedded Systems Engineer Senior Embedded Engineer Embedded Systems Architect

Salary Range

₹6-25 LPA

Industry Demand

Growing demand in IoT, automotive, and consumer electronics due to the proliferation of smart devices and automation.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Intel NXP Semiconductors Tata Elxsi Robert Bosch STMicroelectronics

Work Environment

Work in labs for hardware development and testing, with office-based programming and system integration tasks.
Telecommunications Engineer

Job Description

Telecommunications Engineers design and maintain communication systems, including wireless networks, fiber optics, and satellite systems. They analyze signal performance, optimize network coverage, and ensure reliable connectivity. They also work on emerging technologies like 5G and collaborate with service providers to implement network solutions.

Relevant Subjects

Signals and Systems Digital Electronics

Required Skills

Network Design Signal Processing Wireless Communication Troubleshooting

Career Path

Junior Telecom Engineer Telecommunications Engineer Senior Telecom Engineer Network Architect

Salary Range

₹5-20 LPA

Industry Demand

High demand in telecommunications and IT sectors due to advancements in 5G, IoT, and network infrastructure.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Reliance Jio Bharti Airtel Nokia Ericsson Huawei

Work Environment

Mix of office-based network design and field visits for installation and maintenance of communication systems.
