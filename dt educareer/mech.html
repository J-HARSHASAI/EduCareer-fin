BTech in Mechanical Engineering

Design and innovate the machines that power the future

Course Description

Mechanical Engineering is a dynamic field that focuses on the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems, including machines, engines, and tools. It equips students with the skills to innovate and develop technologies for industries ranging from automotive to aerospace, energy, and robotics.

Relevant Subjects

  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Mechanics of Materials
  • Machine Design
  • Manufacturing Processes
  • Robotics and Automation

Required Skills

  • CAD/CAM (SolidWorks, AutoCAD)
  • Finite Element Analysis
  • Thermodynamic Analysis
  • Prototyping and Testing
  • Project Management
  • Problem Solving
  • Team Collaboration

Job Roles After Completion

Mechanical Design Engineer

Job Description

Mechanical Design Engineers create and develop mechanical systems and components using CAD software like SolidWorks and AutoCAD. They perform simulations, analyze designs for strength and functionality, and collaborate with manufacturing teams to ensure designs are feasible for production. They also ensure compliance with industry standards and optimize designs for efficiency and cost.

Machine Design Mechanics of Materials

Manufacturing Engineer

Job Description

Manufacturing Engineers develop and optimize production processes to ensure efficient and cost-effective manufacturing of mechanical components. They design workflows, select appropriate machinery, and implement automation solutions. They also monitor production quality and troubleshoot issues on the factory floor.

Thermal Engineer

Job Description

Thermal Engineers specialize in the design and analysis of systems involving heat transfer, such as engines, HVAC systems, and power plants. They use thermodynamic principles and simulation tools to optimize energy efficiency and thermal performance. They collaborate with design and manufacturing teams to ensure systems meet performance and safety standards.

Job Description

Job Description

Automotive Engineers design and develop vehicles, focusing on components like engines, transmissions, and chassis systems. They work on improving vehicle performance, fuel efficiency, and safety while integrating advanced technologies like electric and autonomous systems.
