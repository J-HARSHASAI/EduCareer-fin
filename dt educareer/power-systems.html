MTech in Power Systems

Master the art of designing and managing efficient power systems

Course Description

The MTech in Power Systems focuses on the design, analysis, and management of electrical power systems, including generation, transmission, distribution, and integration of renewable energy sources. This program provides in-depth knowledge of power system protection, smart grid technology, and energy management, preparing graduates for roles in the energy and power sector.

Relevant Subjects

  • Power System Analysis
  • High Voltage Engineering
  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Power System Protection
  • Smart Grid Technology
  • Electrical Machines and Drives
  • Energy Management Systems

Required Skills

  • Proficiency in power system simulation tools (e.g., MATLAB/Simulink, PSS/E, ETAP)
  • Understanding of power system stability and control
  • Knowledge of renewable energy integration (solar, wind, etc.)
  • Familiarity with smart grid technologies and IoT applications
  • Expertise in high voltage engineering and protection systems
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Project management and teamwork skills
