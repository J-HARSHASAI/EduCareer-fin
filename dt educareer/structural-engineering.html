MTech in Structural Engineering

Master the art of designing resilient and sustainable structures

Course Description

The MTech in Structural Engineering focuses on the design, analysis, and construction of structures like buildings, bridges, and dams, ensuring safety, sustainability, and resilience against natural forces. This program provides in-depth knowledge of structural dynamics, earthquake engineering, and advanced material technologies, preparing graduates for roles in civil engineering and infrastructure development.

Relevant Subjects

  • Structural Dynamics
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • Earthquake Engineering
  • Advanced Concrete Technology
  • Steel Structures
  • Bridge Engineering
  • Geotechnical Engineering

Required Skills

  • Proficiency in structural analysis software (e.g., STAAD.Pro, SAP2000, ANSYS)
  • Understanding of structural dynamics and seismic design
  • Knowledge of material properties (concrete, steel, etc.)
  • Finite element analysis for structural simulations
  • Project management and teamwork skills
  • Familiarity with building codes and standards (e.g., IS codes, Eurocodes)
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills
