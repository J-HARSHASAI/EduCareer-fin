BTech in Electronics and Communication Engineering

Connect the world with advanced communication systems and cutting-edge electronics

Course Description

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) focuses on the design, development, and application of electronic devices, circuits, and communication systems. This field equips students with skills to innovate in areas such as wireless communication, embedded systems, VLSI design, and signal processing, driving advancements in telecommunications, IoT, and consumer electronics.

Relevant Subjects

  • Digital Signal Processing
  • VLSI Design
  • Communication Systems
  • Microprocessors and Microcontrollers
  • Analog Electronics
  • Embedded Systems

Required Skills

  • Circuit Design (Cadence, PSpice)
  • Embedded Programming
  • Signal Processing
  • VLSI Design Tools
  • Wireless Communication
  • Problem Solving
  • Team Collaboration

Job Roles After Completion

VLSI Design Engineer

Job Description

VLSI Design Engineers design and develop integrated circuits and chips using tools like Cadence and Synopsys. They create layouts, perform simulations, and optimize designs for performance, power, and area. They collaborate with fabrication teams to ensure chip functionality and compliance with industry standards.

Relevant Subjects

VLSI Design Analog Electronics

Required Skills

Cadence Synopsys Chip Design Verification

Career Path

Junior VLSI Engineer VLSI Design Engineer Senior VLSI Engineer Chip Design Lead

Salary Range

₹7-25 LPA

Industry Demand

High demand in semiconductor, consumer electronics, and automotive industries due to the need for advanced chip designs.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Intel Qualcomm Texas Instruments MediaTek Samsung

Work Environment

Primarily office-based for design and simulation, with occasional collaboration with fabrication facilities for testing and validation.
Embedded Systems Engineer

Job Description

Embedded Systems Engineers design and program embedded systems for applications like IoT devices, automotive electronics, and consumer products. They write firmware, program microcontrollers, and integrate hardware and software components. They also perform debugging and testing to ensure system reliability and performance.

Relevant Subjects

Embedded Systems Microprocessors and Microcontrollers

Required Skills

Microcontroller Programming Firmware Development RTOS Debugging

Career Path

Junior Embedded Engineer Embedded Systems Engineer Senior Embedded Engineer Embedded Systems Architect

Salary Range

₹6-22 LPA

Industry Demand

Growing demand in IoT, automotive, and consumer electronics due to the rise of smart devices and automation.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Robert Bosch Tata Elxsi NXP Semiconductors STMicroelectronics Honeywell

Work Environment

Work in labs for hardware development and testing, with office-based programming and system integration tasks.
Signal Processing Engineer

Job Description

Signal Processing Engineers develop algorithms to analyze and process signals for applications like audio, video, and telecommunications. They use tools like MATLAB and Python to design filters, enhance signal quality, and implement real-time processing systems. They collaborate with product teams to integrate signal processing solutions into devices.

Relevant Subjects

Digital Signal Processing Communication Systems

Required Skills

Signal Processing MATLAB Python Algorithm Development

Career Path

Junior Signal Processing Engineer Signal Processing Engineer Senior Signal Processing Engineer Signal Processing Specialist

Salary Range

₹6-20 LPA

Industry Demand

Strong demand in telecommunications, audio processing, and medical imaging due to advancements in signal processing technologies.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Qualcomm Texas Instruments Bose Philips Nokia

Work Environment

Primarily office-based for algorithm development and simulation, with lab work for testing and integration.
Telecommunications Engineer

Job Description

Telecommunications Engineers design and maintain communication systems, including wireless networks, fiber optics, and satellite systems. They analyze signal performance, optimize network coverage, and ensure reliable connectivity. They also work on emerging technologies like 5G and collaborate with service providers to implement network solutions.

Relevant Subjects

Communication Systems Digital Signal Processing

Required Skills

Network Design Wireless Communication Signal Processing Troubleshooting

Career Path

Junior Telecom Engineer Telecommunications Engineer Senior Telecom Engineer Network Architect

Salary Range

₹5-20 LPA

Industry Demand

High demand in telecommunications and IT sectors due to advancements in 5G, IoT, and network infrastructure.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Reliance Jio Bharti Airtel Nokia Ericsson Huawei

Work Environment

Mix of office-based network design and field visits for installation and maintenance of communication systems.
RF Engineer

Job Description

RF Engineers design and optimize radio frequency systems for wireless communication, including antennas, transmitters, and receivers. They analyze RF performance, conduct field tests, and ensure compliance with regulatory standards. They also work on technologies like 5G and IoT, collaborating with network teams to enhance connectivity.

Relevant Subjects

Communication Systems Analog Electronics

Required Skills

RF Design Antenna Design Network Optimization Testing Tools

Career Path

Junior RF Engineer RF Engineer Senior RF Engineer RF Systems Architect

Salary Range

₹6-22 LPA

Industry Demand

Growing demand in telecommunications, aerospace, and defense due to advancements in wireless technologies and 5G networks.

Companies Providing These Jobs

Qualcomm Nokia Ericsson Reliance Jio ISRO

Work Environment

Mix of office-based design and simulation with field testing to optimize RF systems and network performance.
