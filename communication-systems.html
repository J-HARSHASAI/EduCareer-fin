MTech in Communication Systems

Master the art of designing and optimizing communication networks

Course Description

The MTech in Communication Systems focuses on designing and optimizing communication networks, wireless systems, and signal processing for applications in telecommunications, IoT, and satellite systems. This program provides in-depth knowledge of wireless communication, digital signal processing, and network security, preparing graduates for high-demand roles in the telecom and networking industry.

Relevant Subjects

  • Wireless Communication
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Optical Communication
  • Network Security
  • Antenna Design
  • Satellite Communication
  • Internet of Things (IoT) Systems

Required Skills

  • Understanding of communication protocols (e.g., TCP/IP, LTE, 5G)
  • Signal processing and analysis
  • Proficiency in MATLAB or similar tools
  • Knowledge of RF (Radio Frequency) systems
  • Network design and optimization
  • Familiarity with IoT protocols and systems
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills
