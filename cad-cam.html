MTech in CAD/CAM

Master the art of computer-aided design and manufacturing

Course Description

The MTech in CAD/CAM focuses on computer-aided design (CAD) and computer-aided manufacturing (CAM) for applications in mechanical engineering, product design, and industrial automation. This program provides in-depth knowledge of 3D modeling, finite element analysis, CNC programming, and robotics, preparing graduates for specialized roles in manufacturing and design industries.

Relevant Subjects

  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD)
  • Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM)
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA)
  • Robotics and Automation
  • CNC Programming
  • Product Lifecycle Management (PLM)
  • Advanced Manufacturing Processes

Required Skills

  • Proficiency in CAD software (e.g., AutoCAD, SolidWorks, CATIA)
  • Knowledge of CAM software (e.g., Mastercam, Siemens NX)
  • Understanding of CNC machining and programming
  • Finite element analysis for structural design
  • 3D modeling and simulation
  • Problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Familiarity with manufacturing processes
