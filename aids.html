BTech in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Harness the power of AI and data to drive innovation

Course Description

Artificial Intelligence and Data Science is a specialized branch that combines AI techniques with data science methodologies to extract insights from data, build intelligent systems, and solve complex problems using advanced analytics and machine learning.

Relevant Subjects

  • Machine Learning
  • Data Mining and Warehousing
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Statistics and Probability
  • Deep Learning
  • Data Visualization
  • Programming (Python, R)

Required Skills

  • Programming (Python, R)
  • Data Analysis (Pandas, NumPy)
  • Machine Learning (Scikit-learn, TensorFlow)
  • Statistical Modeling
  • Data Visualization (Tableau, Matplotlib)
  • Big Data Tools (Hadoop, Spark)
  • Problem Solving and Critical Thinking

Job Roles After Completion

Data Scientist

Job Description

Data Scientists leverage advanced analytical techniques to uncover actionable insights from large and complex datasets, enabling organizations to make data-driven decisions. They build predictive models, perform statistical analyses, and develop machine learning algorithms to solve business problems such as customer segmentation, fraud detection, and demand forecasting. Additionally, they communicate findings through compelling data visualizations and reports, collaborating with stakeholders to implement strategies that drive growth and efficiency.

Relevant Subjects

Statistics and Probability Data Visualization

Required Skills

Python R Machine Learning Data Visualization

Industry Demand

High demand across industries like finance, healthcare, retail, and technology due to the growing need for data-driven decision-making.

Work Environment

Typically work in office settings or remotely, collaborating with data engineers, business analysts, and executives. The role often involves working on cross-functional teams with a mix of independent analysis and team meetings.

Career Path

Junior Data Scientist Data Scientist Senior Data Scientist Chief Data Officer

Salary Range

₹10-30 LPA

Companies Providing These Jobs

Google Amazon Microsoft IBM Accenture
AI Engineer

Job Description

AI Engineers design, develop, and deploy artificial intelligence systems that mimic human intelligence to solve real-world challenges. They work on creating and optimizing machine learning models, deep learning algorithms, and neural networks for applications like natural language processing, computer vision, and autonomous systems. AI Engineers also integrate these models into scalable production environments, ensuring they perform efficiently while collaborating with software developers and domain experts to deliver innovative solutions such as chatbots, recommendation systems, and predictive analytics tools.

Relevant Subjects

Machine Learning Deep Learning

Required Skills

TensorFlow PyTorch Python Software Engineering

Industry Demand

Rapidly growing demand in tech, automotive, healthcare, and gaming industries due to the increasing adoption of AI technologies.

Work Environment

Often work in tech-focused environments, either in offices or remotely, with a mix of coding, model training, and collaboration with product teams. The role may involve working on cutting-edge projects with tight deadlines.

Career Path

Junior AI Engineer AI Engineer Senior AI Engineer AI Architect

Salary Range

₹8-25 LPA

Companies Providing These Jobs

Google Microsoft Amazon IBM TCS
Data Analyst

Job Description

Data Analysts play a critical role in transforming raw data into meaningful insights that drive business decisions. They collect, clean, and analyze data from various sources, using statistical tools and visualization techniques to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies. Data Analysts create reports and dashboards to present their findings to stakeholders, helping organizations optimize processes, improve customer experiences, and increase profitability. They often work closely with business teams to understand requirements and provide actionable recommendations, such as identifying market opportunities or improving operational efficiency.

Relevant Subjects

Data Mining and Warehousing Big Data Analytics

Required Skills

SQL Excel Tableau Python

Industry Demand

Strong demand in sectors like marketing, finance, e-commerce, and consulting, as businesses increasingly rely on data to gain a competitive edge.

Work Environment

Usually work in office settings or remotely, focusing on data analysis and reporting. The role involves regular interaction with business teams and may require presenting findings in meetings or to clients.

Career Path

Junior Data Analyst Data Analyst Senior Data Analyst Data Scientist

Salary Range

₹4-15 LPA

Companies Providing These Jobs

Deloitte Accenture Wipro Infosys Capgemini
